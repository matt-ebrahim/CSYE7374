Course Description

This course provides a hands‑on introduction to state‑of‑the‑art generative AI for healthcare. Students will build and experiment with advanced techniques—including latent‑space diffusion models for conditional synthetic medical‑image generation—and gain working knowledge of large language models (LLMs) and modern natural‑language‑processing workflows.

Key topics include:

  • Diffusion and latent‑diffusion models for synthesizing medical data in low‑resource domains (e.g., rare diseases)
  • Text‑to‑image and image‑to‑text pipelines, enabling multimodal analysis and reporting
  • Retrieval‑augmented generation (RAG) and chatbot architectures for clinical knowledge retrieval and decision support
  • Safe, bias‑aware, and regulatory‑compliant deployment of generative models in real healthcare settings

By the end of the course, students will be able to design, train, and critically evaluate cutting‑edge generative AI systems to tackle complex challenges in medical imaging, drug discovery, and other healthcare applications.
